# Skill: zoom-out

## Purpose
Step back from a local decision and evaluate whether it fits the wider system, product, and roadmap.

## Use when
- A proposed fix feels too local.
- The team may be optimizing the wrong layer.
- You need to check alignment with architecture and product direction.

## Process
1. State the local problem.
2. State the broader system goal.
3. Identify affected domains.
4. Identify short-term vs long-term trade-offs.
5. Compare options.
6. Recommend a direction.

## Output format
```md
# Zoom-out Review

## Local Problem
## Broader Context
## Affected Domains
## Options
## Trade-offs
## Recommendation
## What To Do Now
## What To Defer
```

## Rules
- Do not use zoom-out to avoid making a decision.
- End with actionable next steps.
- Separate “now” from “later”.
