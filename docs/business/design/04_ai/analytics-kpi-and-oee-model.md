## Design rule

KPI/OEE framing should remain compatible with:
- discrete machine/station views
- line-level supervisory views
- later process/batch resource and campaign views

## Examples
- availability / downtime views
- performance / throughput views
- quality/accepted-good views
- bottleneck and delay drivers
