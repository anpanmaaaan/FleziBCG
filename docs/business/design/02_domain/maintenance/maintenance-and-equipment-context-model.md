## History

| Date | Version | Change |
|---|---|---|

Status: Maintenance/equipment context note.

## Purpose

This domain framing exists to keep equipment/resource context first-class across the platform.

## Key rule

Equipment is not user identity.
It is execution and maintenance context.

## Scope later

- equipment master and hierarchy
- availability/maintenance adjacency
- capability and eligibility context
- links to Station Execution, process/batch execution, and digital twin views
