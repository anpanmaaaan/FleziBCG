## Purpose

Supervisory Operations owns:
- plant/line/station visibility
- operation/run detail visibility
- escalation and intervention views where policy allows
- Gantt/timeline perspectives

## Alignment rule

Supervisory surfaces must monitor session-owned execution truth.
They must not invent parallel ownership semantics in the frontend.
